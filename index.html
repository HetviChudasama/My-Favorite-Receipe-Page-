Samosa Recipe

A crispy and delicious Indian snack loved by all!

Why I Love Samosas

Samosas remind me of family gatherings and celebrations. They are crispy, filled with flavorful potatoes, and perfect with tea!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour with water and knead into dough.
  2. Prepare the stuffing by saut√©ing potatoes, peas, and spices.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles and fill with stuffing.
  4. Fold into a triangle and seal the edges.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with chutney.

Enjoy your crispy homemade samosas!
